About Me

Hi, my name is Syeda and I am a full-stack web developer with a passion and drive to keep learning new skills and keep evolving in both personal and professional life. Having lived in multiple countries and experiencing different cultures, I have developed a deep understanding about the importance of adaptability and communication.

Websites are a way of communication. They’re increasingly becoming the most effective way of reaching a larger audience. A developer’s job is not just to create a website that serves a purpose but to give the audience an insight into the brand and all that it stands for. I believe my understanding of people, their emotions and how they see the world gives me an imagination and creativity that helps in uniting these two seemingly distinct worlds of computer and the society.

My drive to learn and equip myself with skills and tools that would help me translate my ideas into meaningful products led me to enroll in the UCLA Extension coding program. During the program I have created various front-end, back-end and full-stack applications. Working on my projects, I have developed a passion for programming and found a new understanding of communicating with a computer utilizing programming languages and realized how the whole process feels natural to me.

Since recently graduating from UCLA coding Bootcamp, I find myself well equiped with experience and knowledge to built variety of applications and confident in my ability to learn any new language or technology. I am well versed in HTML/CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, React, Node, Express, MySQL, MongoDB, Git more. Currently looking to start my career at an entry-level position hoping to grow as a front or backend developer for a well-established and trusted organization.
